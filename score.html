Best Practices

    Standards

    • 1-5: Poor - You are just starting to get the hang of things. Keep practicing and refining your skills. Focus on basic concepts and build a strong foundation.
    • 6-10: Fair - You have a good understanding of essential principles but still have room for improvement. Work on deepening your knowledge and applying your skills to more complex problems.
    • 11-15: Good - You have a solid grasp of the subject and can apply your knowledge effectively in practical scenarios. You're on the right track to becoming an expert, but continue learning and exploring advanced topics.
